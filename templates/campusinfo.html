A DIAMOND IN THE ROUGH


Coimbatore city offers businesses the best of many worlds. Compact, convenient and surrounded by professional educational institutions, the city is a powerhouse of talent. It has been ranked as India’s best emerging city, along with other accolades for investment friendliness and growth. Well connected by air, road and rail Coimbatore is also home to varied industries ranging from textile to healthcare. The second largest & fastest developing city in Tamil Nadu and one among hundred to be developed as a ‘Smart City’, Coimbatore accounts for 6 Universities, 47 Arts & Science, 66 Engineering, 3 Medical & 2 Dental Colleges, 25 Polytechnics and 27 Management Institutions. The region produces more than 30,000 engineering graduates every year. Bestowed with salubrious climate throughout the year, the city is home to around 25,000 MSMEs, demonstrating harmonious industrial relations scenario.

L&T Coimbatore - A Centre of Manufacturing Excellence


Spread over 220 acres, nestled aside NH-47, L&T Campus at Malumichampatti is the epitome of precision manufacturing excellence. Among its many facets lie facilities for manufacture of high pressure valves, electrical systems and critical components for defence & aerospace sectors. Design & Development centres for power & motor control solutions and construction & mining equipment, coupled with dedicated surface treatment and testing facilities ensure that every product manufactured here meets the highest global standards.
